About the author

Hi, I'm Diego Alvergnat! I was born the 25th of December 2000 in Paris and I moved to Lima-Peru when i was 7. I've always been fascinated by the South American continent and adventures and i thought it was very stupid to go back to France whithout even explore most of the countries that surrounded me for more than 10 years! So when I finished high school and turned 18, I had a lot of free time until classes start in France and I decided to explore the continent by myself and my backpack starting on February 2019. However, due to time constraint, I couldn't visit all the countries. Yet, I visited Peru, Bolivia, Chile, Argentina, and Brasil alone in 2 months! So if you are temerous and want to do a road trip across South America, keep scrolling down!
